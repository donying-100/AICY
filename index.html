Aicy: Social Media Snake

A fresh take on the timeless classic. Navigate the feed, collect likes, and become the biggest snake online!

About The Game

Say goodbye to boring apples! In Aicy: Social Media Snake, you'll relive the addictive fun of the classic snake game with a modern, viral twist. You are a snake navigating a busy social media feed. Instead of fruit, you'll consume 'likes', 'shares', and 'comments' to grow longer and climb the leaderboard. It's a simple concept with surprisingly deep strategy and endless fun.

How to Play

The goal is simple: become the longest snake in the feed without crashing. The controls are intuitive and designed for quick, responsive action.

  • Control Your Snake: Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to direct your snake.
  • Grow Longer: Navigate towards the social media icons (likes, comments) to 'eat' them and increase your length.
  • Avoid Crashing: Do not run into the walls of the game area or your own tail! One wrong move and it's game over.
  • Aim for a High Score: The longer you get, the higher your score. Challenge yourself and your friends!
