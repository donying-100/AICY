About The Game

Welcome to the intense world of Aicy: Monster Survivors. This is not just a game; it's a test of your will, strategy, and reflexes. You are thrown into a vast arena swarming with monstrous creatures, and your only goal is to survive for as long as possible. With every monster you defeat, you'll gain experience, level up, and unlock powerful new abilities and weapons. Each run is a new adventure, offering unique upgrade paths and challenges.

How to Play

Getting started with Aicy is simple, but mastery is a true challenge. Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to navigate your character through the hordes. Your character will attack automatically, so your focus should be on strategic positioning and dodging enemy attacks.

  • Move: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character.
  • Collect Gems: Defeated enemies drop experience gems. Collect them to level up.
  • Upgrade: When you level up, you'll be presented with a choice of new skills or upgrades. Choose wisely to build your power!
  • Survive: The longer you last, the stronger the enemies become. Adapt your strategy and hold out as long as you can.
